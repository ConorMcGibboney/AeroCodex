# Code of Conduct

AeroCodex is a technical engineering project. Discussions should be rigorous, evidence-based, and respectful.

Participants are expected to:

- critique equations, code, assumptions, tests, and data rather than people;
- disclose uncertainty and limitations clearly;
- welcome correction when a source, derivation, unit, tolerance, or implementation is wrong;
- avoid harassment, threats, insults, discriminatory remarks, or personal attacks;
- keep safety-critical claims conservative and evidence-backed.

Maintainers may moderate, edit, hide, or remove contributions that violate these expectations. Serious or repeated violations may result in loss of participation privileges.
